Emmanuel Mkare Mlewa

Accounts Consultant | Audit & Tax Expert | Strategic Financial Management

Mkare Emmanuel Mlewa is a Certified Public Accountant (CPA-K) with a BCom in Accounting from the University of Nairobi and over 8 years’ experience in finance and accounting across sectors like energy, healthcare, and audit. He currently servesas the ICPAK Mombasa County Representative, championing professional development, mentorship, and ethical leadership.

His expertise includes financial reporting, tax compliance (PAYE, VAT, CorporationTax), budgeting, and audit readiness. Emmanuel is highly skilled in aligning financial strategies with organizational goals, ensuring compliance with Kenyan regulatory standards, and optimizing systems using tools like Microsoft Dynamics 365, QuickBooks, and Opera.

He has also earned certifications in financial modeling, forensic investigations, and anti-money laundering, enhancing his ability to provide strategic, transparent, and risk-aware financial solutions.

As a leader, Emmanuel is known for promoting inclusive initiatives that empower young professionals and bridge the skills gap through mentorship and partnerships. His contributions continue to strengthen the Mombasa professional community and position it as a hub of excellence within ICPAK.

At SifaSkills, Emmanuel offers practical, real-world insights to entrepreneurs and professionals looking to build strong, compliant, and sustainable financial systems. His calm leadership, ethical approach, and focus on continuous learning make him a trusted guide in today’s evolving business landscape.

Joseph Zia

Team Logistics & Trade Facilitation Specialist

Joseph Zia is a seasoned logistics and trade facilitation professional with over 10years of experience in customs clearance, regional freight operations, and supply chain management. He has overseen a broad spectrum of responsibilities across import, export, and transit procedures, including bonded cargo handling, customs documentation, valuation, classification, warehousing, and fleet coordination.

With deep expertise in key trade systems such as Simba 2005, KenTrade, RECTS, and the URA Help Tool, Joseph is well-versed in the East African Community Customs Management Act (EACCMA), the East African Common External Tariff (EAC-CET), and Harmonized System (HS) code classification. His hands-onknowledge of trade systems and regulatory frameworks allows him to offer practicalsolutions to cross-border trade and compliance challenges.

At SifaSkills Consultancy, Joseph plays a pivotal role in translating industry knowledge into impactful training. He develops and delivers hands-on programs thatequip individuals and small businesses with practical logistics and trade skills.
